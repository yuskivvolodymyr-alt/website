‚ö° Chain Abstraction Protocol (CAP)

CAP will become a key technology of Qubetics, providing simple and secure cross-chain operations without relying on traditional bridges.
The user selects the action they want to perform, and the decentralized Solver network automatically determines the optimal way to execute it across multiple blockchains.

During cross-network operations, assets will remain in a secure environment until the transaction is confirmed.
This minimizes risks associated with transferring funds between different networks and makes the process more reliable and transparent.

In the future, CAP will allow cross-chain actions to be executed "in one click", delivering true Web3 interoperability.
This will become the foundation that unites leading ecosystems into a single Qubetics platform.
